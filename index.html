A Survey on Zero-Knowledge Proof in Blockchain

Sep. 2024

In this survey, I examined zero-knowledge proofs (ZKPs) in blockchain, focusing on how non-interactive ZKPs enhance privacy and security in corporate networks by protecting identity and transaction privacy.

Robotic Maze Solver

C, C♯, Mplab, Altium Designer, SolidWorks

2017– 2018

Designed and built a robot in a team of two members, that can autonomously navigate a maze and perform multiple tasks; such as heat and visual victim detection and dropping rescue kits for them. After the robot searches the whole maze, it returns to the starting point by the shortest path possible.
